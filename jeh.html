To my One and only mother


Happy Mother's Day, Mama. 💖

I know dili ko perfect nga anak. Daghan ta'g misunderstandings,
ug mga wada mopa nakapasakit sa usag usa. Pero despite
awaying, one thing I will never question is my love and
sacrifices for us. Thank you, Ma, for giving everything you can
even when life gets hard. Ikaw tong klase sa mama nga willing
mo give up sa imong own happiness para lang mahimong atay
among needs ug para lang okay mi. Usahay dili nako masabtan
dayon imong kakapoy ug imong silent battles, pero as I grow
older, mas masabtan na nako unsa ka lisod mahimong mama.

Thank you for staying strong for us even when you were tired.
Thank you for loving us even during the moments nga lisod mi
higugmaon. Bisan masakitan ka sa among words usahay, wala
nimo gihapon ka nibuhi. You still chose to care for us, support us,
love us unconditionally. Mama, a huge part of who I am today is
because of you. The strength I have, the way I care for people,
and the way I keep fighting even when life gets hard - I learned
all of that from you. You taught me things that no school or book
ever could.

Sometimes makahuna-huna ko sa tanan nimong sacrifices nga
wala nako nakita. The sleepless nights, the worries, the tears
you kept to yourself, and the dreams you probably set aside for
our family. It hurts realizing that while we were growing up,
ikaw diay quietly ga sacrifice sa tanan para namo. I may not say
this often, but I see you now, Ma. I see your efforts. I see your
pain. I see your love in every little thing you do for us every
single day. Sorry for all the times nga wala tika nasabtan. Sorry
sa moments nga mas nasuko kaysa naminaw. Sorry if I made
you feel unappreciated. But please know that deep inside my
heart, I will always be grateful to have you as my mother.

No matter how many arguments we have, ikaw gihapon akong
mama, akong komport, ug akong home. And no matter where life
takes me, dala-dala nako tanan nimong sacrifices ug lessons. I
promise one day, Ma, I'll make you proud. I'll do my best to give
back all the love and sacrifices you gave us. You deserve a life
full of peace, happiness, and love because you spent your whole
life giving those things to us first. Thank you for everything,
Mama. Thank you for never giving up on us.

I love you more than words could ever explain
